# The Negative Effect: How to Invert Image Colors

Sometimes, the best way to see an image in a new light is to turn its colors inside out. Inverting an image swaps every color for its opposite on the color wheel—turning bright whites into deep blacks, and cool blues into warm oranges. * **Creative Expression:** Creating "negative" versions of photos is a classic artistic technique that gives your images a surreal, otherworldly feel.
* **UI & Accessibility Testing:** Developers use color inversion to check how their website components look in different high-contrast modes.
* **Template Creation:** Inverting an image is often the first step in creating advanced masks or silhouettes for graphic design projects.
