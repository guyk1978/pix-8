# Correcting Perspective: Fixing Lens Distortion Instantly

Have you ever taken a photo of a building or a horizon line, only to notice that it looks slightly curved or "warped"? This is called Lens Distortion. While sometimes it adds character, often it ruins the professional feel of architectural photography. Our [Lens Distortion Corrector](/tools/enhancement/lens-corrector) fixes these geometric flaws with precision.

## Understanding the Distortion
* **Barrel Distortion:** The edges of the image seem to bulge outward, like a barrel. This is very common in wide-angle lenses and smartphone cameras.
* **Pincushion Distortion:** The edges appear to pull inward, toward the center. This is common in zoomed-in telephoto lenses.
