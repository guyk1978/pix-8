# Adding Impact: How to Add Text to Images Like a Pro

An image may be worth a thousand words, but sometimes it needs a few extra ones to get the message across. Whether you're creating a social media quote, adding a price tag to a product, or building a hero banner for your website, the [Text Overlay Tool](/tools/editor-studio/text-overlay) is designed to make it simple and professional.

## Why Placement and Typography Matter
Adding text to an image is a balancing act. If the text clashes with the background, your message gets lost.

* **Contrast is King:** Always ensure there is enough contrast between your text and the background. If the background is busy, use a semi-transparent dark or light overlay behind your text.
* **Hierarchy:** Make the most important part of your text the largest. Don't try to cram too much information into one image.
* **Alignment:** Use consistent alignment to guide the reader's eye. Centered text feels formal, while left-aligned text is easier to read for long sentences.
