## How Does AI Background Removal Work?
Modern AI models for image segmentation are trained on millions of images. These models learn to distinguish between the "foreground" (the primary subject) and the "background" by analyzing color patterns, edges, depth of field, and textures.

When you upload your photo to our tool, the AI performs these steps:
1. **Edge Detection:** It identifies the sharp borders of your subject.
2. **Subject Masking:** It creates a "mask" that separates the subject from the rest of the pixels.
3. **Refinement:** It smoothens the edges to ensure the transition looks natural, even around complex areas like hair or fur.

## Tips for the Best Results
* **High Contrast:** The AI works best when there is a clear contrast between your subject and the background.
* **Lighting:** Good lighting helps the AI identify edges more accurately.
* **Complexity:** While the AI is incredibly smart, very busy backgrounds with similar colors to the subject might require a little extra focus from you.
